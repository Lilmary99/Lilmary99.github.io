Brief Synopsis

Our identified problem was scheduling conflicts that are typically encountered by professionals who work remotely. The focus is email service calendars and the need to merge them. There are already some popular calendar mergers in existence but those are too complex, not as accessible, and only allow users to merge calendars from email accounts from the same email service. Our proposed solution is a new interface that is more convenient and more flexible than the existing calendar mergers since it will allow users to merge at least two different calendars from email addresses from at least two different email services.
